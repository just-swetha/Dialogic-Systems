What We Do

Complete Security & Communication Solutions

From a single camera to a full site-wide surveillance and communication system — we handle everything.

📷

CCTV Camera Systems

HD and IP surveillance cameras with remote viewing. Indoor/outdoor, day & night vision, DVR/NVR recording for homes, offices, and warehouses.

📞

EPABX / PBX Systems

Internal telephone exchange systems for offices and businesses. Intercom integration, auto-attendant, call routing, and extensions across floors.

👆

Biometric Attendance

Fingerprint and card-based attendance machines with automatic payroll-ready reports. Ideal for offices, factories, and schools.

🚪

Face ID Access Control

Facial recognition door access systems for secure entry to offices, server rooms, and restricted areas. Contactless and accurate.

🚨

Burglar Alarm Systems

Motion sensors, door/window contacts, and siren alarms with SMS and phone alerts. Protect your premises around the clock.

🌐

Wi‑Fi & LAN Networking

Structured LAN cabling, Wi-Fi access points, and network setup for fast, reliable connectivity across your office or campus.
